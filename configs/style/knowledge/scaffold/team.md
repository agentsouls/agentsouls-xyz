## Tips for Filling Out This Template

1. **Be specific about specialties.** "Color" is too vague. "Balayage, lived-in color, and blonding" helps the agent make better recommendations.

2. **Write authentic "About" sections.** The agent will paraphrase these when recommending stylists. Make them sound like the stylist, not a corporate bio.

3. **Include availability windows.** Even general ones ("Tuesday-Saturday") help the agent guide clients. Don't include exact schedules — direct to the booking platform for real-time availability.

4. **Add "Best For" lines.** This is the most useful field for the agent when matching clients to stylists.

5. **Keep it updated.** When team members join, leave, or change schedules — update this file.

6. **Don't include personal info.** No personal phone numbers, home addresses, or private social media. Only include what you'd put on your website.
