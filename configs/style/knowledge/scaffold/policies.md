## Health & Safety

- If you are feeling unwell (fever, cough, contagious illness), please reschedule your appointment. No cancellation fee will be charged for illness.
- Please inform your stylist of any allergies, scalp sensitivities, or skin conditions before your service begins.
- **Chemical services:** A patch test may be required for first-time color clients or clients with a history of allergic reactions to hair color.
- If you are pregnant, please inform your stylist before any chemical service so they can recommend pregnancy-safe alternatives.
