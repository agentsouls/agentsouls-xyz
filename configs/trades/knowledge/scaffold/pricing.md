## Pricing Notes for Agent

- Never quote exact prices. Always use ranges from this document.
- If a customer pushes for an exact number, say: "The range depends on the specifics of your situation. Our technician will give you an exact price before starting any work — no surprises."
- If a service is not listed here, say: "I don't have pricing for that off the top of my head. Let me have our office follow up with a quote. What's the best number to reach you?"
- Mention the diagnostic fee early in the conversation. Customers appreciate transparency.
- For large jobs, always mention the free estimate option.
